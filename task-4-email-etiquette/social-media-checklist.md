# 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language in posts and comments  
- Share informative, positive, and meaningful content  
- Keep your privacy settings updated and secure  
- Verify facts before sharing any information  
- Build a positive digital identity that reflects your personality and goals  

---

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information (phone number, address, passwords)  
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Do not spread fake news, rumors, or unverified information  
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or toxic discussions  
- Do not copy or use others’ content without proper credit  

---

## 🔐 Stay Responsible Online
Social media is a powerful tool that can impact your personal and professional life. Always think before you post and ensure your online behavior is safe, respectful, and responsible.

---

## 🚀 Reminder
**Your digital footprint is permanent — use it wisely.**
